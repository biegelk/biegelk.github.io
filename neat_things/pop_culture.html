Historical pop culture

I have a fascination with forgotten articles of popular culture from ages past. Say, pre-1920 or so.

With a bit of legwork, these works offer a marvelous, highly specific doorway into another time. Once you get to grips with some cultural context, you can step through to find yourself in conversation with a real, often very vibrant and opinionated person from a previous era. And, to put it in a very self-centered way, since most of these works aren't widely discussed today, those conversations often feel enchantingly private, as if you're sharing a fun secret across the ages with some author or artist.
